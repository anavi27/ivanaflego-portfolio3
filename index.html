About me

Hi, I’m Ivana Flego, a Master’s student in Computer Science at the University of Rijeka. My passion lies in crafting seamless UI/UX designs and developing responsive, user-friendly websites. I thrive on challenges and embrace every opportunity to learn and grow, both individually and within a team.
As a creative thinker, I love blending innovation, functionality, and uniqueness in all my projects. My goal is to create digital experiences that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and impactful. Whether brainstorming fresh ideas or fine-tuning the smallest details, I’m driven by a deep enthusiasm for problem-solving and bringing concepts to life.
I have 7 years of experience in Abobe Photohop which I've started learning in highschool as a hobby, but fell in love with it's endless posibilities. I'm very skilled in Figma, which I've used for many projects and my Bachelor thesis was "UI/UX Guidelines for Mobile Applications". In terms of frontend development I use HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I've also created a few apps (during my studies) in Flutter which I also love.

I’m excited about working in environments that encourage collaboration, spark curiosity, and push the boundaries of design and technology.
